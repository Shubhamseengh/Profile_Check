National Defence Academy (NDA) Examination

Your gateway to becoming an Officer in the Indian Armed Forces.

About NDA

The NDA examination is conducted twice a year by UPSC for candidates who wish to join the Army, Navy, and Air Force wings of the National Defence Academy and Naval Academy.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Age: 16.5 to 19.5 years
  • Education: 10+2 pass
  • For Air Force & Navy: Physics and Mathematics mandatory
  • Gender: Both male and female candidates eligible
  • Nationality: Indian

Exam Pattern

  • Mathematics: 300 Marks
  • General Ability Test (GAT): 600 Marks
  • Total Written Marks: 900
  • SSB Interview: 900 Marks

Syllabus

Mathematics: Algebra, Trigonometry, Calculus, Statistics, Probability

GAT: English, Physics, Chemistry, History, Geography, Polity, Current Affairs

Selection Process

  1. Written Examination
  2. SSB Interview (5 Days)
  3. Medical Examination
  4. Final Merit List

Training Academies

  • National Defence Academy (Pune)
  • Indian Naval Academy (Ezhimala)
