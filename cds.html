Combined Defence Services (CDS) Examination

Your gateway to becoming a Commissioned Officer after Graduation.

About CDS

The CDS examination is conducted twice a year for graduates who aspire to join the Indian Armed Forces as commissioned officers in Army, Navy, and Air Force.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Age: 19 to 25 years (varies by academy)
  • Education: Graduation from a recognized university
  • For Air Force: Physics & Mathematics at 10+2 level
  • Nationality: Indian

Exam Pattern

For IMA, INA & AFA

  • English – 100 Marks
  • General Knowledge – 100 Marks
  • Elementary Mathematics – 100 Marks
  • Total – 300 Marks

For OTA

  • English – 100 Marks
  • General Knowledge – 100 Marks
  • Total – 200 Marks

Syllabus

English: Grammar, Vocabulary, Comprehension

General Knowledge: History, Polity, Geography, Economics, Current Affairs

Mathematics: Arithmetic, Algebra, Geometry, Trigonometry, Statistics

Selection Process

  1. Written Examination
  2. SSB Interview (5 Days)
  3. Medical Examination
  4. Final Merit List

Training Academies

  • Indian Military Academy (Dehradun)
  • Officers Training Academy (Chennai)
  • Air Force Academy (Hyderabad)
  • Indian Naval Academy (Ezhimala)
